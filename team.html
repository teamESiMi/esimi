Team

We're a growing team working in Austin, TX. If you're interested in hearing more about our team, or would like to join us along our journey, drop us a line at hello@esimi.co.

Daniel Apo, Ph.D. Founder & CEO Dan is a product of the University of Benin, MIT, and Virginia Tech. Dan's an energy harvesting expert with experience running 2 successful businesses, and is the holder of 3 patents.
Dwayne Macik, Ph.D. CTO Dwayne has his Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering from Texas A&M. Dwayne is the interface between ESiMi and designers, suppliers, and manufacturers, while owning our development methodology - but really, he spends most of his time tinkering with PCBs.
Lyndon Core Marketing Manager Lyndon was born-and-raised Australian, graduated from the Economics Program at the University of Maryland, and has previous startup and web development experience. He's currently living-and-breathing our Kickstarter campaign.
Geoff Arroyo Social Media Intern Geoff is on track to graduate with an MIS degree from the University of Texas. He has previous experience as a writing consultant, and spends his free time managing his music blog.
Maddie Harrison Engineering Intern Maddie is a mechanical engineering student at the University of Texas. She's in the process of becoming a rockstar design engineer!
